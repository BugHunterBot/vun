Editions

 

Wing FTP Server is available in 4 editions: Free Edition, Standard Edition, Secure Edition, and Corporate Edition.

You can download the installer file from our official website. If you never install WingFTP before, then you can have a 30-day trial period with full functions. After 30 days, it will become Free Edition automatically if you never register.

Please note, Free Edition is only for non-commercial use (personal use), and the other three editions can get FREE email and remote assistance support for a lifetime.
